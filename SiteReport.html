Experience & Learning:

It was a fascinating experience for me to work on this assignment. There were many ups and downs along the way. Due to this, I have learned a lot, from styling & designing to making responsive hamburger icons. Most of the time had problems with making my webpage responsive, whereas the styling and the content remained the same. As for the style, I wanted to be simple but effective designs. Easy to view, and which is easy to navigate through. As it is said:

“Think more, Design less"

-Ellen Lupton

Ups:

  • From this assignment i got to know my limits and skills.
  • I got to learn new codes, style and improve my skills.
  • Learning how to debug an error.
  • Learning how to use git-hub and its branches.

    • Downs:

  • A small mistake can change the whole website.

    • Conclusion:

    From this assignment I got to improve my skills, creativeness
