Experience & Learning:

It was a fascinating experience for me to work on this assignment. There were many ups and downs along the way. Due to this, I have learned a lot, from styling and designing to making responsive hamburger icons, menu. Most of the time had problems with making my webpage responsive, whereas the styling and the content remained the same. As for the style, I wanted to be simple but effective designs. Easy to view, and which is easy to navigate through. As it is said:

“Think more, Design less"

-Ellen Lupton

From this assignment, I got to learn more things and expand my knowledge. As the success and the final outcome of this Assignment, I would like to thank our Module Leader, Sir, Ankit Thapa for providing me basic ideas and for being there for us whenever we ran into any problems.At last, I would like to conclude, with the help of the given project I got an in-depth knowledge of HTML and CSS.

Ups:

  • From this assignment i got to know my limits and skills.
  • I got to learn new codes, style and improve my skills.
  • Learning how to debug an error.
  • Learning how to use git-hub and its branches.

Downs:

  • A small mistake can change the whole website.

Conclusion:

From this assignment I got to improve my skills, creativeness
